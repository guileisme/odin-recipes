Huge Scorpion and Walking Mushroom Hotpot

Description

A warm, nourishing dish created by Senshi on his first time meeting Laios' adventuring group, made from ingredients found on the first floor of the dungeon. The leftover soup is later used to make a Man-Eating Plant Tart.

Ingredients

  • Huge Scorpion
  • Walking Mushroom
  • Mushroom feet
  • Seaweed (artic moss, star jelly) (as needed)
  • Invertatoes (about 5 med. size)
  • Dried slime (to preference)
  • Water (as needed)

Steps

  1. Scorpion, mushroom, algae, tubers and dried slime noodles are all boiled together in water, creating a light soup.
