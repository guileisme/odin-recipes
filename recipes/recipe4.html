Mandrake and Basilisk Omelet

Description

A dish created by Senshi consisting of a fluffy Basilisk egg omelet filled with minced Basilisk bacon and mandrake. If the mandrake used was killed with its 'head' still attached, it will be less bitter and more mellow.

Ingredients

  • Mandrake (1 med. size)
  • Basilisk bacon (use the fattiest cuts) (50 grams)
  • Basilisk egg
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • Ketchup (to taste)

Steps

  1. Watch episode 2 of Dungeon Meshi or read manga's chapter 4!
